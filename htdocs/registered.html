Welcome to FreePlus Accounts!
Thank you for registering for FreePlus Accounts.
You have (or shortly will have) received an activation email from us.  Please follow the instructions contained within that email as it is how we verify that you are who you say you are.  It also ensures that, once you have confirmed your registration, no one else can get access to any of your details without knowing both your password and your memorable word.
If you do not receive your email within the next hour or so please check your junk mail or spam folder as your email application may place the message there.
We hope that you find FreePlus Accounts useful and as simple to use as as we believe it to be.  If you have any questions or problems (of a technical nature) using the system please contact us using our Contact page and we will endeavour to respond within one working day.
When you log in for the first time you will be asked to enter basic details about your company and, if you are already in business, your opening balances.  If applicable these details include your Company Registration Number, VAT Registration Number, VAT Quarter End and Accounting Year End and so it would be helpful to have them to hand.
In the mean time why not check out some of the video tutorials (if you have not already done so) to see how easy it is to use FreePlus Accounts.
