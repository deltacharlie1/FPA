About FreePlus Accounts

How it started – FreePlus Accounts began as an in-house accounting system for Corunna Systems based in Cambridgeshire, UK. Corunna Systems’ managing director, Doug Conran had tried just about every accounting package, from the market leading applications to the Open Source freebies, and found them all either too difficult to use or too complex for the requirements of a small business.

Fed up with paying accountants’ high fees, Doug decided the only solution was for Corunna Systems to develop its own accounting software.

The free, online accounting service – Corunna Systems realised that it had developed a system that would be of value to any small business, so Doug teamed up with Sean Proctor to establish FreePlus Accounts and make the system more widely available as a free, online service.

FreePlus Accounts is an accounting package designed for start-ups, micro businesses and sole traders. The system is designed and created from the perspective of the business manager. It uses the terminology that any business owner might use, not necessarily that of an accountant, and the functionality is designed specifically to match the needs of the business, not accountants.

How the service can help you – if you are the owner or manager of a small business, keeping the accounts up to date can seem a bit of a chore, especially if you are working with overcomplicated software or having to design your own spreadsheets.

Try FreePlus Accounts absolutely free for at least 6 months so that you can try out:

  • managing your accounts more easily
  • keeping them up to date at all times, and
  • having access to them from virtually anywhere, not just when you are sat at your PC.

and, as a bonus, you get to try all the paid for features for the first 3 months!
