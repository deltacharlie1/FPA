Seven Reasons to use FreePlus Accounts:

  1. It’s free…. Yes really. Not a free trial, but free to use. A very modest monthly subscription gives more facilities but the free service works for basic accounts.

  2. It’s an on-line, web based system (cloud computing is here) that runs on the FreePlus server so it’s accessible from any location with an internet connected PC. So, no software to load and maintain on your PC as you always have the latest version and all you need is a browser.

  3. It’s safe and secure. There’s no data or software held on your PC, it’s all on the FreePlus server so there are no backups, off-site data storage or program updates to worry about. A two stage log on process helps to keep your access and data secure.

  4. It’s intuitive. As a non-accountant I like the simplicity and lack of accounting jargon with functions such as ‘Pay money out’ which I can understand. It’s obviously a help to know what a nominal ledger is but the accounting terminology is used sparingly and only where necessary.

  5. It’s flexible. Invoices can be designed to be the shape you want and contain the detail you want (with a subscription) or you can use the default layout, with or without a logo. Invoices, credit notes and quotations can be printed and posted or e-mailed from within the system. There are standard reports to view or print and some analysis reports with selection criteria to list items according to date, type, reference, etc.

  6. It’s approved by The Institute of Certified Bookkeepers so I can be confident the accounting process is sound and VAT returns will be correct, which gives me peace of mind.

  7. It’s fully supported. Questions are answered, there’s FAQs, a forum to post queries, a user manual to download and the support staff are friendly, competent and know the system, and it’s all provided at no extra cost.

So, if you want an easy to use, functional accounts package for a sole trader or small business, it’s waiting for you at www.freeplusaccounts.co.uk/
