What FreePlus Accounts gives you

 

FreePlus Accounts provides a fully functional, totally free, online accounting system for any small business, including start-ups, micro-businesses and sole traders. There is no time or use limit. Once youâ€™ve signed up you can use the service as often as you want and for as long as you want with no charge.


You have the option of signing up for some or all of the additional system features and there is a modest charge for each of these. You will not need to pay for any additional features to make the system usable and you are not required to take up any of the extra functionality if you do not want it. You will not be asked to make a subscription to any of the extra services and there are no automatic renewals.

The core FreePlus Accounts system

  • Unlimited access to your accounts for a single user from anywhere you can connect to the internet
  • Up-to-date chart of accounts listing all your assets and liabilities against nominal codes
  • Tracking of loans and loan repayments
  • Sales
    • Complete customer list
    • Ability to create standard invoices and send them as emails to your customers or to yourself for printing
    • List of all sales invoices, with outstanding invoices separately identifiable
    • Tracking of payments received against invoice
  • Purchasing
    • List of all your suppliers
    • List of purchase invoices, with outstanding invoices separately identifiable
    • Log of purchase invoices
    • Tracking of expenses
    • Tracking of payments against expenses and purchase invoices
  • Banking
    • List of bank accounts and transactions against each (payments and receipts)
    • History of account statements
    • Records of bank account withdrawals, deposits and transfers
    • Bank reconciliations
  • VAT
    • Automatic calculation of figures for UK VAT returns (form VAT 100)
  • Reporting
    • Summary account status and outstanding invoices
    • Analysis of invoices and transactions by criteria you set
    • Trial balances and profit/loss statements
    • Aged debtors/creditors
