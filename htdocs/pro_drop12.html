Information

The fourth menu in this series of dropdown menus that use full png to give a 3D translucent effect to the sub menus.

This one one expands on the previous menu to give a method of producing variable width sub menus. In this demo there are 3 different widths of sub menu but it can be adapted have more.

This also has a small run off area in which the mouse can go outside of the menu a little way before it collapses.

In this example IE6 has its own special style for the background images which enables it to correctly display the alpha image transparency.

Tested in IE6, IE7, IE8, Firefox, Opera, Safari (PC) and Google Chrome Beta

This menu is probably the most complex one produced to date and as such will require a donation to support CSSplay if you wish to use it.
